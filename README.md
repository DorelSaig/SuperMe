#  <p align="center"> Welcome to SuperMe! </p>
</br>

## :handshake: About
SuperMe, is an Hebrew native **Shared Grocery Lists Application**.
The app was built to give a solution to the old fashion way of writing on paper/built-in phone notes. 
SuperMe will help you keep track of what you are purchasing and what is left to be purchased.
you can **create**, **edit** and **share** a list and always be sure the list is showing you **real time data**.

## :sparkles: Features

 - **Login and Sign-up** with any valid phone number 
 - Chose/Change your **profile picture**
 - **Create as many grocery lists** you need
 - Browse through the **built in categories and groceries** in the app, and **add your own items** if you can not find what you looking for.
 - **Share the list** with your siblings, partner, friends and any one who have the app by their phone number.
 - Easily manage your lists, with our **friendly UI**.
 - **Swipe to remove** items from a list
 - **Long press to delete** lists
 - Send your list's participants notifications when **"Going to shopping"**, **"List Updated"** or **"List is Closed"**
